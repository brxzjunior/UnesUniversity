Quem somos

Unes University

With almost five decades and more than 500 thousand students, Estácio is one of the largest and most respected groups in the education sector in Brazil. In face-to-face teaching, it operates in 23 states and the Federal District, totaling around 90 Units. In Distance Learning, we are present throughout Brazil through our more than 1,000 Digital Learning centers.

Our institutions and courses are recognized by the MEC with high quality concepts. We invest in a nationalized teaching model, with curricula aligned with the needs of the job market, and also in technology and innovation as differentials to improve learning.

In Higher Education, we offer Undergraduate and Postgraduate courses (lato sensu and stricto sensu), in addition to corporate solutions and Extension Courses.

Active in projects that contribute to the social and cultural development of the country, we promote the Corporate Social Responsibility Program “Educar para Transformar”, supporting actions and projects in five pillars – Sport, School, Citizenship, Culture, Innovation and Entrepreneurship. These initiatives reflect our commitment to offering accessible and quality education and, thus, generating a positive impact for the construction of a more just society.
